Somnath Jyotirling

Somnath temple has been a jyotirlinga site for the Hindus, and a holy place of pilgrimage. The site is also called Prabhasa ("place of splendor"). It is one of five most revered sites on the seacoast of India, along with the nearby Dwaraka in Gujarat, Puri in Odisha, Rameswaram and Chidambaram in Tamil Nadu.
